Shelby Wardlaw - running for NTT Faculty Vice President

"It has been a pleasure serving you as VP for NTTs since 2023. In the past two years I have served on the Executive Council, the Legislative Committee, the By-laws Committee, the NTT Working Group and the Mary Gibson Internship Committee. I started and chaired the NTT Working Group in 2023, intending to create a space for NTT issues and advocacy. The group has now transitioned into the NTT Contract Taskforce, and is working on a wish-list of contract amendments for our next bargaining campaign. In the fall of 2024, I restarted the Mary Gibson Internship for Undergraduate Activists. The internship is now funding five wonderful undergraduates as they work on projects to improve our community.

In addition, I have held three office hours a semester to hear from NTTs directly. I’ve kept folks updated with monthly newsletters. I have hosted NTT promotional information sessions, answered contract questions, attended protests, canvassed for local union-friendly politicians and coordinated with other union leaders to push back on the detrimental cuts to our Writing Program.

There is a lot to be done – now more than ever – and I would be honored to continue this important work. I believe in transparent governance and a member-driven union. During my second term, I would continue to live out these values in leadership."
